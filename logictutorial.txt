# FILL GAME DART: COMPLETE LOGIC TUTORIAL

Welcome to the official logic guide for Fill Game Dart. This tutorial covers everything from basic hits to advanced batch strategies and bonuses.

---

## 1. THE GAME STRUCTURE (BATCHES)

The game is played in two distinct phases called "Batches".

### BATCH 1: THE QUALIFICATION (221.5 PTS)
- **Goal**: Be the first to reach or exceed the target score of **221.5 points**.
- **The Bar**: Once a player hits 221.5, Batch 1 ends. Their final score becomes "The Bar" that the opponent must try to beat in Batch 2.

### BATCH 2: THE FINAL RACE (BEAT THE BAR)
- **Goal**: Reach a score higher than your opponent's final Batch 1 score.
- **Victory**: The first player to surpass their specific target wins the entire game immediately.

---

## 2. THE DARTBOARD TARGETS

### DIRECT NUMBER HITS (1-14)
- You can target numbers 1 through 14 directly.
- Each hit progresses your "Personal Completion" for that number (e.g., Number 10 requires 10 hits to complete).

### RING HITS (GROUPS)
Hitting a ring is a powerful move that awards hits to **ALL** numbers in that group:
- **Ring 1 (Inner)**: 14, 13
- **Ring 2**: 12, 9, 5, 10
- **Ring 3**: 11, 1, 3, 8
- **Ring 4 (Outer)**: 7, 4, 2, 6

---

## 3. THE SCORING SYSTEM

### A. FILLER POINTS (+2 PER HIT)
- **Numbers 2-14**: Every hit grants +2 pts, capped at `(Number Value * 2)`.
- **Number 1 SPECIAL**: Awarded **ONLY ONCE per batch**. Your first hit on #1 in Batch 1 gives pts; subsequent hits in Batch 1 give 0 filler pts. The same applies to Batch 2.

### B. TOP FILLER BONUS (+7 PER NUMBER)
- **Applicable**: Numbers 2 - 14.
- **Condition**: Awarded once **BOTH** players have hit more than half of the number's requirement.
- **Winner**: The player with the most hits on that number gets +7 pts.
- **Tie**: Both players get +3.5 pts each.

### C. FILL-UP BONUS (+10 PER NUMBER)
- **Number 1 SPECIAL**: Awarded **ONLY ONCE per batch**. Your first hit on #1 in Batch 1 gives +10 pts; subsequent hits in Batch 1 give 0. The same applies to Batch 2.
- **Numbers 2-14**: Awarded to the player who "closes" the number for the whole board (completes it for themselves after the opponent has already finished).

---

## 4. NUMBER 1 SUMMARY: THE "12-POINT RULE"
- **Batch 1 (First Hit)**: Grants **+12 total points** (2 Filler + 10 Fill-Up).
- **Batch 1 (Later Hits)**: Grants **0 points**.
- **Batch 2 (First Hit)**: Grants **+12 total points**.
- **Batch 2 (Later Hits)**: Grants **0 points**.

---

## 5. EXAMPLE SCENARIO: A RING 3 STRIKE (BATCH 1)

If you hit **Ring 3** (Numbers 11, 1, 3, 8):
1. **Hits Recorded**: You get +1 hit on all numbers.
2. **Scoring for #1**: If this is your first hit on #1 in Batch 1, you get **+12 pts**.
3. **Scoring for Others**: You get +2 pts for #11, +2 pts for #3, and +2 pts for #8 (assuming they aren't capped).
4. **Total**: 12 + 2 + 2 + 2 = **18 points** from one ring hit!

---

## 6. PRO TIPS
- **Don't Waste Darts on #1**: Since it only pays out once per batch, don't keep aiming for it (directly or via Ring 3) once you've collected your 12 points.
- **Batch 1 Strategy**: Aim for high numbers to build a massive "Bar" that your opponent will struggle to beat in Batch 2.
