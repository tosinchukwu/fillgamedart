# GAME LOGIC UPDATE

## Key Rules
- **Per-Hit Scoring**: Every hit calculates points independently.
  - **Filler**: +2
  - **Top-Filler (Lead)**: +7
  - **Top-Filler (Tie)**: +3.5
  - **Top-Filler (Trailing)**: +0
- **Global Completion**: Numbers close based on the **total combined hits** of both players.
  - **Fill-Up Bonus**: +10 to the player who lands the final hit.

## Examples
- **Number 12 (Lead)**: A,A,B,B,A,A,A,A(A) -> +2 (Filler) + 7 (Lead) = **9 pts**.
- **Number 9 (Trailing + Closure)**: B,B,B,A,A,B,B,A(A) -> +2 (Filler) + 10 (Fill-Up) = **12 pts**.
- **Number 5 (Lead + Closure)**: A,A,B,B(A) -> +2 (Filler) + 7 (Lead) + 10 (Fill-Up) = **19 pts**.

# Fill Game Dart - Ring Strategy Logic

## Ring Composition
- **Ring 1 (Innermost)**: 14, 13
- **Ring 2**: 12, 9, 5, 10
- **Ring 3**: 11, 1, 3, 8
- **Ring 4 (Outermost)**: 7, 4, 2, 6

## Impact Rule
- A hit on any Ring (1, 2, 3, or 4) acts as a **direct hit on EVERY number** contained within that ring's group.
- Each hit adds to the hit count of the respective numbers and is recorded as an ordinal hit position (1st, 2nd, etc.) on the scoreboard.

## Scoring Logic per Number (1st to 14th Dart)

### 1. Filler Points (+2 Pts)
- **Numbers 2-14**: Every hit grants +2 filler points, capped at `(Number Value * 2)`.
- **Number 1**: Special Rule - **Every hit** grants +2 filler points (Uncapped).

### 2. Top Filler Bonus (+7 Pts)
- **Applicable to**: Numbers 2 through 14.
- **Condition**: Awarded only once **BOTH** players have hit more than half of the number's value.
- **Winner**: The player with the higher total hit count on that specific number receives +7 pts.
- **Tie**: If hit counts are equal, both players receive **+3.5 pts**.

### 3. Fill-Up Bonus (+10 Pts)
- **Number 1**: Special Rule - **Every hit** grants +10 Fill-Up points.
- **Numbers 2-14**: Awarded only when a hit **completes board closure** for that specific number (both players finished).

## CALCULATION EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Hit on Ring 1 (Numbers 13, 14)
Scenario: Player A hits Ring 1. 
- **For Number 13**: 
  - +2 Filler points.
  - If Player A now has more hits than Player B (and both > 6.5): +7 Top Filler points.
  - If Number 13 was already completed by Player B, this hit closes it: +10 Fill-Up points.
- **For Number 14**: 
  - +2 Filler points.
  - If Player A leads hits: +7 Top Filler points.
  - If this hit closes Number 14 for the board: +10 Fill-Up points.
- **Grand Total for this single dart**: (2+7+10) + (2+7+10) = **38 points** (if all conditions met).

### Example 2: Hit on Ring 3 (Numbers 11, 1, 3, 8)
Scenario: Player A hits Ring 3.
- **Number 1**: only grants **+12 pts** (2 Filler + 10 Fill-Up) immediately n is once till the batch end.
- **Number 11**: +2 Filler (plus potential +7 TFP or +10 Fill-Up).
- **Number 3**: +2 Filler (plus potential +7 TFP or +10 Fill-Up).
- **Number 8**: +2 Filler (plus potential +7 TFP or +10 Fill-Up).
- **Grand Total**: 12 + (2+0+0) + (2+0+0) + (2+0+0) = **18 points** (minimum case).

### Summary Note
Every interaction counts. A single ring hit can trigger multiple bonuses across all numbers in that ring group simultaneously, adding a massive strategic advantage.
