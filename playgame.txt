# Fill Game: Official Rules & Strategy

## 🎯 Game Objective
Strategically complete numbers 1-14 on a shared board to earn filler points and lucrative bonuses. Win by dominating the Batch winning system.

## 🏹 Turn Structure
- Players alternate turns.
- Each turn consists of **3 dart throws**.
- "Solo vs CPU" mode follows the same structure.

## 💰 Scoring System
### 1. Filler Points (+2)
- Earn **+2 points** for every hit on a number you haven't personally completed.
- Completion target = the number's value (e.g., hitting #10 ten times completes it).
- **No Excess Points**: Hits beyond the completion target do not award filler points.

### 2. Top Filler Bonus (+7) - Strategic Multiplier
- Awarded for each individual number from **2 to 14**.
- If you have **more hits** on a number than your opponent, you receive **+7 points**.
- **Tie**: If both players have an equal number of hits, the bonus is split (**+3.5 points each**).
- Competition for this bonus continues even after a number is completed personaly.

### 3. Fill-Up Bonus (+10) - The Closer
- Awarded to the **last player** to complete a number.
- This hit "closes" the number for the entire board. Once closed, no more points can be earned from that number by either player.

### ⭕ Ring Mechanics
- The board has 4 strategic rings.
- Hitting a ring impacts **all numbers** within that ring group.
- You gain hits/points for all numbers on the ring that you have not yet personally completed.

## 🏆 How to Win (The Batch System)
The game is played in two distinct phases:

### Phase 1: Batch 1 (Target: 221.5)
- Players race toreach **221.5 points**.
- Batch 1 ends **immediately** when a player reaches or exceeds this score.
- Both players' final Batch 1 scores are recorded (e.g., Player A: 230, Player B: 150).

### Phase 2: Batch 2 (The Final Race)
- The board resets.
- Players now race to surpass the **opponent's Batch 1 score**.
- **Win Condition**: The first player to surpass their target score wins the entire game **immediately**.
- If the board closes before anyone hits their target, the Batch 1 winner is declared the overall champion.

---
*Strategy Tip: Focus on Rings early to build a lead, but watch the Top Filler bonuses on high-value numbers (10–14) to secure a Batch 2 advantage!*
